Support & Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a couple?

To set up a couple, register for an account and then invite your partner through the app. Go to Settings → Couple Setup and follow the prompts to send an invitation to your partner's email address. Once they accept the invitation, you'll be connected as a couple.

What if my partner doesn't receive the invitation?

If your partner hasn't received the invitation email, please check the following:

  • Verify you entered their email address correctly
  • Ask them to check their spam or junk folder
  • Try resending the invitation from the Settings page

If problems persist, please contact our support team.

Can I change my partner's email address?

Yes, if your partner hasn't accepted the invitation yet, you can update their email address in Settings → Couple Setup. If they've already joined, they'll need to update their email from their account settings.

How can I change or update our anniversary date?

You can change your relationship start date in Settings → Relationship Start Date. Select the new date and save your changes.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Settings → Danger Zone → Clear All Data and confirm your choice. Please note that this action is irreversible and will delete all your shared memories and content.

What's the difference between free and premium versions?

The free version of Duet Love includes basic features like sharing memories and sending messages. The premium version unlocks additional features such as unlimited photo storage, advanced date planning tools, relationship insights, and removes ads.

Will my data be backed up?

Yes, all your data is automatically backed up to our secure cloud servers. If you change devices or reinstall the app, simply log in with your account to restore all your couple data.
